# Default Birthsigns

This document lists all the default birthsigns available in Menhir.

---

### The Archer
*Those born under the sign of The Archer are expert marksmen with unparalleled precision.*

**Lore:** The Archer constellation guides arrows true and steady to their mark.

**Passive Abilities:**
- None

**Active Abilities:**
- **Conjure Bow:** Conjure a spirit bow that lasts 60 seconds. (1 use/day)

**Required Mods:** `ebwizardry`, `wizardryutils`

---

### The Beast
*Those born under the sign of The Beast channel primal fury and animalistic strength.*

**Lore:** The Beast constellation embodies raw, untamed power of the wild.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Feast:** Heal 0.5 hearts on every kill.

**Active Abilities:**
- **Roar:** Unleash a ferocious roar that frightens nearby enemies. (1 use/day)

**Required Mods:** `ebwizardry`, `wizardryutils`

---

### The Blaze
*Those born under the sign of The Blaze command the fury of fire and are immune to its touch.*

**Lore:** The Blaze constellation burns eternal, a symbol of passionate fury.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Embercloak:** Gain temporary fire resistance upon taking fire damage. (15 seconds, 3 uses/day)

**Active Abilities:**
- **Flame Strike:** Shoot a powerful fireball. (7 uses/day)

---

### The Charmer
*Those born under the sign of The Charmer possess natural charisma and persuasive abilities.*

**Lore:** The Charmer constellation represents the power of words and influence.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Lucky Charm:** You are luckier (+0.5 Luck attribute).

**Active Abilities:**
- **Charm:** Nearby villages treat you with a boon (Hero of the Village effect). (1 use/day)

---

### The Conjuration
*Those born under the sign of The Conjuration excel at summoning and commanding otherworldly forces.*

**Lore:** The Conjuration constellation binds the paths between realms.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Lingering Magic:** +25% Spell duration.
- **Efficient Magic:** -20% Spell mana cost.

**Active Abilities:**
- **Arcane Echo:** Once per day, if no spell is yet bound, you may bind the held spell book as your Arcane Echo spell. Future activations will cast the bound spell for free, without consuming mana or the scroll. (1 use/day)

**Required Mods:** `ebwizardry`, `wizardryutils`

---

### The Ender
*Those born under the sign of The Ender master the ability to teleport and traverse dimensions.*

**Lore:** The Ender constellation marks those who transcend boundaries of space.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Spatial Slip:** Automatically teleport to safety when falling from a great height. (1 use/day)

**Active Abilities:**
- **Dimensional Step:** Teleport a short distance (up to 32 blocks) in the direction you're looking. (2 uses/day)

---

### The Forge
*Those born under the sign of The Forge are blessed with the ability to craft and repair with supernatural skill.*

**Lore:** The Forge constellation symbolizes creation and the mastery of craft.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Smoldering Strikes:** Attacks have a 20% chance to set the target on fire for 3 seconds.

**Active Abilities:**
- **Rekindle:** Restore 25% durability of a held item. (1 use/day)

---

### The Golem
*Those born under the sign of The Golem possess unbreakable resilience and earthen might.*

**Lore:** The Golem constellation represents unwavering strength and endurance.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Ironbound:** Your maximum health is increased by 15%, but you move 5% slower.

**Active Abilities:**
- **Ironwall:** Gain Resistance II and Slowness I for 15 seconds. (1 use/day)

---

### The Grove
*Those born under the sign of The Grove are one with nature, nurturing life around them.*

**Lore:** The Grove constellation celebrates the eternal cycle of growth and renewal.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Verdant Bond:** Nearby plants grow faster.

**Active Abilities:**
- **Nature's Embrace:** Instantly nourish nearby plants in a 3x3 radius, as if using bonemeal. (1 use/day)

---

### The Hollow
*Those born under the sign of The Hollow walk between life and death, wielding necromantic power.*

**Lore:** The Hollow constellation dwells at the threshold between worlds.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Warding Mark:** Permanently gain the Ward effect, reducing magic damage taken.
- **Diminished Power:** Your spells are 25% less potent.

**Active Abilities:**
- **Spellshatter:** Instantly remove any active potion effect affecting you. (1 use/day)

**Required Mods:** `ebwizardry`, `wizardryutils`

---

### The Lodestone
*Those born under the sign of The Lodestone are anchored to the earth, drawing strength from below.*

**Lore:** The Lodestone constellation draws power from the deep earth.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Earth Anchor:** You are steadfast and resistant to knockback (50% Knockback Resistance).

**Active Abilities:**
- **Waystone:** Sneak+Cast to Mark your current location (Free). Cast normally to Recall/Teleport to the marked location (Costs Charge). Max Range: 500 blocks. (2 uses/day)

---

### The Mage
*Those born under the sign of The Mage possess innate magical prowess and enhanced spell potency.*

**Lore:** The Mage constellation illuminates the path of those who seek arcane mastery.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Arcane Affinity:** Your spells are 10% more potent.

**Active Abilities:**
- **Mana Surge:** Gain Font of Mana for 15 seconds, greatly increasing mana regeneration. (1 use/day)

---

### The Mountain
*Those born under the sign of The Mountain stand unyielding against all adversity.*

**Lore:** The Mountain constellation stands as an eternal monument to perseverance.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Stoneborn:** Gain Haste when underground (Y-level 40 and below).

**Active Abilities:**
- **Torchlight:** Place a torch. (10 uses/day)

---

### The Seer
*Those born under the sign of The Seer possess foresight and the ability to unveil hidden knowledge.*

**Lore:** The Seer constellation reveals truths hidden from ordinary sight.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Threat Sense:** When your health drops below 33%, nearby hostile mobs will start glowing.

**Active Abilities:**
- **Revelation:** Discover an unknown spell from a held, unlearned spell book. (1 use/day)

---

### The Summoner
*Those born under the sign of The Summoner command allies to fight by their side.*

**Lore:** The Summoner constellation calls forth loyal companions from the ether.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Frail Body:** Your maximum health is reduced by 1 heart.

**Active Abilities:**
- **Summon Wolf:** After a short channel, summon a loyal wolf to fight for you. This ability also causes a brief period of Weakness. (3 uses/day)

---

### The Thief
*Those born under the sign of The Thief are swift and cunning, masters of stealth and agility.*

**Lore:** The Thief constellation favors those who move unseen through the shadows.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Featherstep:** Your safe fall distance is increased (no damage under 7 blocks) and you move 3% faster.

**Active Abilities:**
- **Shadowstep:** Become invisible for 10 seconds and gain Speed I for 20 seconds. (1 use/day)

---

### The Warrior
*Those born under the sign of The Warrior are natural combatants, skilled in battle and resilient in defense.*

**Lore:** The Warrior constellation shines brightest in times of conflict, guiding champions to glory.

**Passive Abilities:**
- **Path of War:** Deal 10% more damage with melee weapons and gain 2 armor points.

**Active Abilities:**
- **Berserk:** Gain Strength II and Resistance I for a short duration. (1 use/day)
